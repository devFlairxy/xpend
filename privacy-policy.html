Privacy Policy

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms. This policy applies to all information collected through our website and/or any related services, sales, marketing or events.

Welcome to JustXpend, your AI-powered crypto wallet assistant on WhatsApp. At JustXpend, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

What Information We Collect

When you use JustXpend through WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Your full name, BVN, and phone number (from WhatsApp)
  • Profile photo (if publicly available via WhatsApp)
  • Bank account details for transfers
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses

Transaction & Usage Data

  • Voice notes and messages you share
  • Details of transactions (e.g., amount, recipient, purpose, network)
  • Deposit requests and payment confirmations
  • Time, date, and frequency of interactions
  • Exchange rate queries and calculations
  • Balance inquiries and transaction history

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your cryptocurrency deposits and transfers
  • Provide real-time exchange rates and calculations
  • Verify your identity and comply with KYC requirements
  • Process payments to Nigerian bank accounts
  • Enhance user experience with personalized responses
  • Improve our AI's understanding of voice and text inputs
  • Ensure security, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting
  • Comply with legal obligations and financial regulations
  • Generate transaction receipts and confirmations

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

Voice & Message Processing

When you send voice notes or messages, our system processes them using machine learning tools to extract relevant financial information (e.g., transfer amounts, recipient details, deposit commands). These inputs may be used to train and improve our AI, but personal identifiers are anonymized or removed where possible.

All communications are encrypted using WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption.

Data Storage & Security

We use secure servers and encryption to protect your data. Access to user information is strictly controlled and only available to authorized personnel for support or system improvement.

We store your data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with laws, or improve system performance. You may request data deletion at any time.

Cryptocurrency transactions are recorded on the blockchain, which is publicly accessible but does not contain your personal information.

Third-Party Services

JustXpend integrates with third-party services including:

  • NOWPayments for cryptocurrency processing
  • WhatsApp Business API for messaging
  • Payment processors for bank transfers
  • Exchange rate providers
  • Analytics platforms

We ensure these services comply with standard data protection practices. We do not share your data with them unless required for service delivery or compliance.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Correct or update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent (which may limit your use of JustXpend)
  • Export your transaction history
  • Request information about our data processing practices

To make any of these requests, simply email us at privacy@justxpend.ai.

Children's Privacy

JustXpend is not intended for use by individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware of such use, we will delete the data immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you through WhatsApp or via our website.
